Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

At Jebena Cafe, we believe in the art of brewing the perfect cup of coffee.

Our journey began in 2020 with a simple mission: to create a cozy space where everyone feels at home. From our carefully sourced beans to our warm, inviting atmosphere, every detail is crafted with love.

We take pride in using 100% organic, ethically sourced coffee beans, ensuring every sip delivers a rich and authentic taste. Our skilled baristas bring passion and precision to every cup, making each visit a unique experience.

  • Freshly brewed from premium beans
  • Handcrafted espresso drinks
  • Homemade pastries & snacks
  • Comfortable workspace with Wi-Fi
  • Community-focused environment
  • Open daily 7AM - 10PM
